Building Indigenous AI Together

Change happens through community. Join Indigenous peoples, allies, and technologists working to transform AI for everyone.

Our Vision

We envision AI systems that reflect the full spectrum of human knowledge and experience, where Indigenous voices are not just included but centered in shaping technology that serves all communities.

Indigenous Leadership

Indigenous peoples lead their own tech initiatives with support, not extraction, from allies.

Cultural Sovereignty

Communities maintain control over their cultural data and how it's used in AI systems.

Reciprocal Relationships

Technology development based on mutual benefit rather than one-way extraction.

Indigenous AI Organizations & Initiatives

Indigenous AI Collective

A network of Indigenous technologists, researchers, and community leaders working to decolonize AI development.

Focus: Research, Advocacy, Community Building

Native Land Digital

Uses technology to map Indigenous territories and educate people about Indigenous presence and history.

Focus: Digital Mapping, Education

Indigenous Protocol & AI

Research initiative examining Indigenous perspectives on AI governance and data sovereignty.

Focus: Policy, Governance, Research

Te Hiku Media

Maori media organization developing AI tools for Indigenous language revitalization.

Focus: Language Technology
