Why Are We Missing from the AI Revolution?

Indigenous peoples, our languages, our art, and our culture are being left behind by artificial intelligence. The great knowledge machines of our time are missing the wisdom of the first peoples.

7,000+ Indigenous languages worldwide
40% At risk of disappearing
Less than 1% Represented in AI training data

The Problem

Erasure by Algorithm

AI systems are trained on data that systematically excludes Indigenous voices, perpetuating digital colonialism and cultural erasure.

Lost Knowledge

Thousands of years of traditional knowledge, oral histories, and cultural wisdom are absent from the datasets shaping our digital future.

Biased Representation

When Indigenous peoples are included in AI, it's often through harmful stereotypes or colonial perspectives rather than authentic voices.

What's at Stake

When AI fails to include Indigenous perspectives, we lose more than data points. We lose:

  • Millennia of environmental knowledge and sustainable practices
  • Rich linguistic diversity that shapes how we understand the world
  • Artistic traditions and cultural expressions
  • Alternative ways of knowing and being
  • Solutions to contemporary challenges rooted in traditional wisdom
